A bit more about me.

Hello, my name is Blenn. I’m a UX Designer, and I’m here to help you create a meaningful experience.

I believe that all experiences in life have a purpose, and coming from a diverse background helped me learn how to be organized, efficient, curious, and adaptable to new situations. I draw on these skills as a UX/UI Designer when creating a full experience for the user. Have a problem? Let’s find a solution!

When I’m not designing, you can find me watching all the TV shows I can get my hands on, bullet journaling, doing way too much online shopping, or simply enjoying the California sun.

Broll Wine

Business Website Design

Introduction

Broll Mountain Vineyards had been looking to redesign their outdated website for years. With the remodel of their wine store in Murphys, CA, they also needed a fresh new way to showcase their strong brand image online.

Working within the new branding guidelines, I created wireframes for a single-page site that would match the more modern aesthetic of the brand. Much of the focus of this site was about organizing content to show off the client's new image as well as to drive traffic to the physical store.

The owners had one request, to use “large beautiful photographs.” I utilized that concept as the centerpiece of the design, building each section to illustrate the brand through the use of the gorgeous photography done at the vineyard.

Conclusion

Working with the client, we chose the best sections from each wireframe to create the perfect website. In the end, the client was thoroughly impressed with how much the site design conveyed the modern feeling they wanted for their web presence.

Once the design was finalized, it was turned over to engineering to be built. The finished product can be viewed at BrollWine.com.
